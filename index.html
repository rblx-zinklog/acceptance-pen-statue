Certificate of Acceptance
of Payment Tendered in Full
❧ ❧ ❧
Be it known to all persons present and hereafter, that this solemn and binding Certificate doth hereby attest and proclaim, with all due authority vested in the undersigned offices, that payment for the acquisition of the celebrated and most distinguished
Object of Purchase
The Pen Statue
— A Most Singular & Exceptional Artefact of the Statuary Arts —
hath been received, verified, and accepted in full by the duly appointed custodians of this Registry, the sum rendered being as follows:
Sum Tendered & Received
$200,001.00 AUD
Two Hundred Thousand and One Australian Dollars, being the Whole and Complete Price, no further monies owing
The aforesaid payment having traversed many a league — as the noble camel crosses the vast and trackless desert — hath arrived in good order at its rightful destination. The Pen Statue shall henceforth pass entirely into the possession and custody of the Purchaser, free of all liens, encumbrances, and prior claims, as is proper under the Laws of Commerce and Good Faith.
Let no man, institution, or sovereign authority hereafter dispute the lawfulness of this transaction. This Certificate, bearing the marks and seals of the parties below, shall stand as irrefutable testimony before any court, council, or tribunal throughout the known world.
